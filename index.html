01 / Origins

The Story
Of Gurkha

"Some names are created. Some names are earned."

For generations, the name Gurkha has represented courage, resilience, discipline, loyalty, and an unwavering spirit that refuses to back down from challenge.

Its origins trace back to the hills of Gorkha, Nepal — a land known for producing individuals whose determination and character became respected far beyond their homeland.

02 / Heritage

Global
Legacy

Over time, the Gurkha name travelled across continents. From Nepal to the United Kingdom and beyond, it became recognized as a symbol of bravery, honor, commitment, and the confidence to move forward when others hesitate.
